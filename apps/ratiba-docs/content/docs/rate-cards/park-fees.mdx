---
title: Park fees
description: National park and reserve entry fees, by category and season.
---

Park fees are per-person, per-day government charges to enter a national park, reserve, or conservation area. They are usually the largest non-accommodation line on a safari quote.

## Park entries

Each park has:

- **Name.** "Serengeti", "Ngorongoro Crater", "Lake Manyara", "Maasai Mara National Reserve", etc.
- **Country.** For multi-country operators.
- **Default residency category.** Usually "Non-resident adult".
- **Notes.** Internal.

## Fee rows

For each park, you add rate rows by:

- **Season.** Optional. If left blank, the rate applies year-round.
- **Residency category.** One of:
  - Non-resident adult
  - Non-resident child
  - East African resident adult
  - East African resident child
  - Citizen adult
  - Citizen child
- **Rate.** Per-person, per-day.
- **Currency.** Often USD for non-resident, local currency for residents/citizens.

The pricing engine picks the right row based on:

- The park visited that day.
- The pax's residency category (set on the trip).
- The day's season.

## Ancillary park charges

In addition to the per-person entry fee, some parks charge:

- **Concession fees** (per-room or per-vehicle, per night) for lodging inside the park.
- **Camping fees** (per-person, per-night) for mobile camps.
- **Vehicle entry fees** (per-vehicle, per-day or once per visit).
- **Crater service fees** (Ngorongoro-specific).

Add these in the **Ancillary** section of each park. Each ancillary has its own charge basis:

- Per vehicle per day
- Per vehicle once per visit
- Per person per night
- Per room per night

The pricing engine applies them automatically based on the day's accommodation and vehicle.

## Pre-seeded parks

When you sign up and pick a country, Ratiba pre-loads the major parks for that country (Tanzania, Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Botswana, South Africa, Namibia, Zambia, Zimbabwe). Rates are not pre-loaded because they change yearly and vary by operator agreement.

## Updating yearly

Tanzania and Kenya park fees change every July. We recommend setting a calendar reminder to update both countries on July 1.

The fastest way to update is the bulk-edit dialog: filter by country and category, then enter new rates inline.

We ship updates weekly. Major changes are listed here. Minor fixes and tweaks are not.

## 2026

### May 2026

- **Activity rates.** Activity rates now have their own dedicated rate-card tab, replacing the inline activity pricing in the day editor.
- **Room type as free text.** Room types are now free text instead of a fixed enum, so you can use whatever names your suppliers use.
- **Pricing engine rewrite.** Cost-plus pricing now correctly handles per-room rates with `max occupancy`, ancillary park fees by basis, and meal-plan derivation.

### April 2026

- **AI itinerary assistant.** New bottom-right assistant that drafts itineraries from a brief, using only your library content and current rates.
- **Real-time collaboration.** Cell-level presence markers and live updates across team members editing the same trip.

### March 2026

- **Tours module.** Reusable itinerary templates with public publishing.
- **Pipeline view.** Kanban-style view of the clients module.

### February 2026

- **Custom domains** on proposals. Use your own subdomain.
- **Three proposal themes:** Classic, Editorial, Bold.

### January 2026

- **Public launch** of Ratiba.
- **Core modules:** itineraries, content library, rate cards, clients, proposals.
- **Multi-currency** rate cards with workspace FX.

## Earlier

Ratiba was in private beta from October 2025 through January 2026 with a handful of design-partner operators in Tanzania, Kenya, and Botswana.

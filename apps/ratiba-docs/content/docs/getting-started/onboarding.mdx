title: Onboarding wizard

The first time you log in to a new workspace, Ratiba walks you through a short onboarding wizard. You can skip any step and come back to it later from [Settings](/docs/team).

## Step 1: Company basics

- **Trading name.** Shown on every proposal.
- **Logo.** Square or wide. SVG or PNG, transparent background recommended.
- **Brand colors.** Primary and accent. Used in proposals and PDF exports.
- **Default reporting currency.** Usually USD or EUR.

## Step 2: Default markup

Ratiba's [pricing engine](/docs/concepts/pricing-model) calculates supplier cost from your rate cards and then applies a markup to produce the selling price.

- **Default markup percent.** For example 25% means a $1,000 supplier cost becomes $1,250 to the client.
- **You can override markup** at the trip level, the day level, or per line item. The default is just the starting point for new itineraries.

If you sell on a net basis (commission paid by suppliers, no markup), set this to 0.

## Step 3: Invite your team

Add team members by email. Each member gets one of three roles.

- **Owner** can do anything, including billing and deleting the workspace.
- **Admin** can manage rate cards, content, and invite teammates, but cannot delete the workspace or change billing.
- **Member** can build itineraries and send proposals, but cannot edit rate cards or invite teammates.

You can change roles later from [Team settings](/docs/team).

## Step 4: Seed content

The wizard offers to pre-load:

- A list of major parks and reserves for your country with default park-fee categories.
- A starter season table (typically green, shoulder, and high).

We recommend accepting both. You can edit or delete any of it later.

Ratiba's left sidebar is organized by what you're doing day-to-day.

## Dashboard

Your home page. Shows recent itineraries, recent client activity (proposal views, comments, acceptances), and any tasks needing attention. Start here every morning.

## Itineraries

All trips you've built. Filter by status (draft, sent, accepted, lost) and search by client name. This is where most of your day will happen.

See [Itineraries](/docs/itineraries).

## Clients

A CRM-lite. Each client has contact info, all their itineraries, and a timeline of every proposal sent. Use this to follow up on quotes and track conversion.

See [Clients](/docs/clients).

## Tours

Pre-built itinerary templates you can clone. Use these for "set departure" group tours or for repeat itineraries you sell often.

See [Tours](/docs/tours).

## Content Library

The reusable building blocks: accommodations, activities, images. This is what the itinerary builder pulls from.

See [Content library](/docs/content-library).

## Rate Cards

Supplier costs. Accommodation rates, park fees, vehicles, transfers, activity rates, plus your season definitions and pricing defaults.

See [Rate cards](/docs/rate-cards).

## Settings

Workspace config, team management, branding, billing.

See [Team](/docs/team) and [Billing](/docs/billing).

## Top-right shortcuts

- **Workspace switcher** (top-left) if you belong to more than one workspace.
- **Notifications bell** for proposal opens, comments, acceptances.
- **Profile menu** for your account, password, sign out.

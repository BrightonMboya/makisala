Ratiba is purpose-built for the African safari market. It replaces the patchwork of spreadsheets, Word templates, and PDFs that most operators use today with a single workspace for content, pricing, itineraries, and client proposals.

This documentation walks through every part of the product, from setting up your workspace for the first time to building a multi-day, multi-park safari and sending a live client proposal.

## What you can do with Ratiba

- **Build itineraries** day-by-day with an AI assistant that pulls from your own content library.
- **Price safaris** automatically using rate cards for accommodations, parks, vehicles, and transfers.
- **Send proposals** as branded, interactive web pages, not flat PDFs.
- **Collaborate** with your sales and operations team on the same live trip.
- **Manage clients** end-to-end, from first enquiry to signed proposal.

## Who Ratiba is for

Ratiba is built for inbound safari operators in East and Southern Africa, destination management companies (DMCs), and travel agencies that sell custom safaris. If you build trip itineraries by hand in Word and price them in Excel, Ratiba will save you hours per quote.

## The itineraries list

Open **Itineraries** from the sidebar to see all trips. You can:

- **Search** by client name, trip name, or destination.
- **Filter** by status (draft, sent, accepted, declined, archived) and date range.
- **Sort** by departure date, value, or last-updated.
- **Bulk archive** trips that are done.

The list also shows status indicators:

- **Eye icon** — proposal has been viewed by the client (hover for view count and last-viewed time).
- **Speech bubble** — client has left comments.
- **Green check** — proposal accepted.
- **Red X** — proposal declined.

## Creating a new itinerary

Three ways:

1. **Blank.** Click **New itinerary** and start from scratch.
2. **From a tour template.** Pick from your [tours](/docs/tours) and the days pre-populate.
3. **Clone an existing trip.** Right-click any itinerary in the list and choose **Duplicate**. Useful for repeat business or "build me one like the Smith trip" enquiries.

## Status lifecycle

- **Draft.** Default. Editable, not yet sent.
- **Sent.** A proposal has been published and a link shared. Editable but the published version stays frozen until you republish.
- **Accepted.** Client clicked accept. Pricing is locked; further edits require unlocking.
- **Declined.** Client clicked decline. Read-only.
- **Archived.** Removed from default views. Still searchable.

Statuses move automatically based on client actions, but you can override them manually if needed.
